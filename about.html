Regarding my internships:

I’m currently a third-year student at Northeastern University, majoring in Finance and Data Science. My internships have given me hands-on experience in finance and data analysis. In the summer of 2023, I interned at SAGER, a drone data collection company in Amman, Jordan. I supported fundraising efforts by researching potential investors and preparing financial data, and I performed competitive analysis to refine our investment pitch. Before that, I interned at Bith.TV, where I collaborated with finance and marketing teams to drive content growth strategies. I also tested the company’s new app and website, identifying key issues and suggesting improvements​

My Hobies:

Fishing and water sports have become two of my favorite hobbies. Initially, I was against fishing because of concerns about harming animals. But after trying it last summer, I was hooked—not just on the act, but the entire experience of being at sea. The peacefulness, the thrill of the catch, and the connection with nature changed my perspective completely. Similarly, wakeboarding is a rush that I can’t get enough of. The sense of speed, balance, and freedom while riding the waves is unbeatable. Both activities give me a chance to be near the water, which is where I feel most alive.
